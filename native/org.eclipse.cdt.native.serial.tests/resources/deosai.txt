The Deosai National Park is a high-altitude alpine plain and national park in
northern Pakistan. The Deosai National Park is located between Kharmang, Astore
and Skardu in Gilgit Baltistan. It has an average elevation of 4,114 metres
(13,497 ft) above sea level, making the Deosai Plains one of the highest
plateaus in the world. Actually the second highest plateau after Chang Thang
Plateau Tibet.

The park protects an area of 3,000 square kilometres (1,200 sq mi). It is well
known for its rich flora and fauna of the Karakoram-West Tibetan Plateau alpine
steppe eco region. In spring, it is covered by sweeps of wildflowers and a wide
variety of butterflies.
